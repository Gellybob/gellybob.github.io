MacLander

An instantly accessible Mac status utility, system resource monitor, and built-in application launcher.

Download for macOS
Requires macOS • Free & Open Source

📊 Live Metrics

Keep a sharp eye on your system health. Instantly view live CPU usage, RAM utilization, and battery status details.

📅 Time & Calendar

Never lose track of your schedule. Built-in time tracking and an elegant calendar overlay keep you organized.

🚀 App Launcher

Declutter your desktop dock. Launch any installed Mac application straight from the MacLander utility interface.
